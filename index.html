I'm an English Language Teacher and freelance word-slinger living in Teotitlan de Flores Magon in Southern Mexico.

My PhD from the University of Iowa is in 19th century U.S. literature and evolutionary theory. Passionate about ecology, diversity, technology, and language, I love helping take manuscripts or projects to the next level.

My hobies include graphic art, web design, and playing the mandolin.
